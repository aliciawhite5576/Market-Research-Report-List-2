<p><p>Standalone Broadcast refers to a 5G technology where content is delivered directly from the source to multiple receivers simultaneously, without the need for individual connections. This approach optimizes the distribution of popular content, reducing network congestion and improving efficiency. On the other hand, Mixed-mode Multicast involves combining both broadcast and unicast transmissions. This technology is capable of delivering personalized services to individual users while simultaneously broadcasting popular content to multiple users. It strikes a balance between efficiency and personalization, offering a more versatile and adaptable broadcasting solution for the 5G market.</p></p>
