<p><p>The aerospace life jacket market consists of three main types: foam jackets, inflatable jackets, and hybrid jackets. Foam jackets are made of buoyant foam materials and provide immediate flotation upon contact with water. Inflatable jackets, on the other hand, are designed to inflate upon submersion or manually. They are lightweight and offer more mobility. Hybrid jackets combine foam and inflatable elements, providing both immediate flotation and additional buoyancy when fully inflated. These jackets are ideal for aerospace environments where space and mobility are crucial factors for survival.</p></p>
