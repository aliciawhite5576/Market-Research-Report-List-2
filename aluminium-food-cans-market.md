<p><p>The aluminium food cans market consists of two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by forming the can body through three separate pieces: the sidewall, bottom end, and top end. These cans typically offer more design flexibility and are commonly used for preserving foods. On the other hand, two-piece cans are manufactured from a single piece of metal, forming both the sidewall and bottom end. This type of can is generally more cost-effective and widely used for products like beverages and pet food.</p></p>
