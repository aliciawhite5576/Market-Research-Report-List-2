<p><p>The aluminum spear gun market is comprised of two main types: pneumatic spear guns and band-powered spear guns. Pneumatic spear guns use compressed air to propel the spear forward, while band-powered spear guns rely on rubber bands for propulsion. Pneumatic spear guns are known for their power and accuracy, making them popular among experienced divers. On the other hand, band-powered spear guns are more affordable and easier to maintain, making them a great choice for beginners or those on a budget.</p></p>
