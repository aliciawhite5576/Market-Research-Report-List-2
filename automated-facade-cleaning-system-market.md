<p><p>The Automated Facade Cleaning System market includes two types of robots: contact cleaning robots and non-contact cleaning robots. Contact cleaning robots are designed to physically touch and clean the building facade using brushes or other cleaning tools. They are suitable for removing stubborn dirt and stains. On the other hand, non-contact cleaning robots use advanced technologies like high-pressure water jets or lasers to clean the facade without any physical contact. They are ideal for delicate surfaces and can ensure efficient cleaning without causing any damage or scratches.</p></p>
