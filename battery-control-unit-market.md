<p><p>The Battery Control Unit (BCU) market can be categorized into three types - Distributed, Centralized, and Modular. In the Distributed market, multiple BCUs are installed near each battery pack, allowing for individual control and management. Centralized BCUs, on the other hand, have a single unit that controls and monitors all battery packs in a system. Lastly, Modular BCUs offer flexibility by allowing the integration of multiple units to manage diverse battery configurations. These different market types enable companies to choose the most suitable BCU solution based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
