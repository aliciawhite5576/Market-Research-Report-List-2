<p><strong>The Breast Shields Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular Nipple Shields</li><li>Contact Nipple Shields</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breast shields are available in two main market types: regular nipple shields and contact nipple shields. Regular nipple shields are designed to provide a protective barrier between a mother's nipple and the baby's mouth, aiding in proper latch and reducing discomfort. On the other hand, contact nipple shields are specifically designed for infants who struggle with latching or have difficulty stimulating milk flow. These shields allow direct contact of the baby's mouth with the mother's nipple, promoting better feeding and milk transfer.</p></p>
