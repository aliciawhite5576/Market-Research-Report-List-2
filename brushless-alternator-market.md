<p><p>A brushless alternator is an electrical generator that uses a rotor with permanent magnets instead of brushes to convert mechanical energy into electrical energy. The magnets on the rotor create a magnetic field, inducing a voltage in the stator windings, which generates electric power. Brushless alternators offer numerous advantages over traditional brush-dependent alternators, including higher efficiency, increased reliability, and reduced maintenance requirements.</p><p>The brushless alternator market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years, and this trend is expected to continue during the forecast period.
