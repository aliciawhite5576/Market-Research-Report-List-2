<p><p>The combination steam oven market has two main types: single door and double door. Single door steam ovens have a single opening for both steam and conventional cooking. They are suitable for small kitchens and provide convenience for everyday cooking. On the other hand, double door steam ovens have separate openings for steam and conventional cooking. This allows for independent use of both functions and provides flexibility for different cooking needs. These ovens are typically larger and are ideal for professional kitchens or for those who frequently cook for larger groups.</p></p>
