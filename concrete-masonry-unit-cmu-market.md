<p><p>The Concrete Masonry Unit (CMU) market consists of two main types: Load Bearing Blocks and Non-Load Bearing Blocks. Load Bearing Blocks are designed to support the weight of a structure and are commonly used for exterior walls and building foundations. Non-Load Bearing Blocks, on the other hand, are not meant to support structural weight and are often used for interior walls and partitions. Both types of CMUs are made from concrete and offer durability, fire resistance, and sound insulation properties for construction projects.</p></p>
