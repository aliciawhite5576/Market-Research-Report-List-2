<p><p>Confocal Sensor Systems market includes three main types: Point Sensors, Multipoint Sensors, and 3D Line Confocal Sensors. </p><p>Point sensors measure height variation by focusing on individual points for high precision. Multipoint sensors capture multiple points simultaneously, enabling faster scanning speed. 3D Line Confocal Sensors produce a 3D profile of the measured surface by scanning a line of points. Despite their differences, all types offer accurate measurement and are used in various industries such as automotive, semiconductor, and aerospace for quality control, inspection, and automation applications.</p></p>
