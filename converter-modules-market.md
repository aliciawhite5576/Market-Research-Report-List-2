<p><strong>The Converter Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC-DC Converter Module</li><li>DC-AC Converter Module</li><li>AC-DC Converter Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The converter modules market consists of three main types: DC-DC converter modules, DC-AC converter modules, and AC-DC converter modules. DC-DC converter modules convert a direct current (DC) input voltage into a different DC output voltage. DC-AC converter modules transform DC voltage into alternating current (AC) voltage. On the other hand, AC-DC converter modules convert an AC input voltage into a DC output voltage. These converter modules find applications in various industries, such as telecommunications, industrial automation, and automotive, to efficiently manage and convert power sources.</p></p>
