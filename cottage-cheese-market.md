<p><p>Cottage cheese market types can be categorized into three main categories: natural cheese, processed cheese, and cheese food. Natural cheese refers to the type made from milk, salt, and enzymes without any additional ingredients. Processed cheese is manufactured by combining natural cheese with emulsifiers, stabilizers, and flavor enhancers. Cheese food is a product containing various proportions of cheese and non-cheese ingredients such as emulsifiers, water, and dairy fats. Each market type offers different characteristics and taste profiles, catering to diverse consumer preferences in the cheese industry.</p></p>
