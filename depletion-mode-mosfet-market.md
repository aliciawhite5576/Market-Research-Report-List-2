<p><p>The Depletion Mode MOSFET market includes two types of transistors: P-channel Type MOS and N-channel Type MOS. The P-channel Type MOSFET has a negative threshold voltage with a normally ON operation, meaning the channel is conducting by default. It acts as a switch when a positive voltage is applied to its gate. On the other hand, N-channel Type MOSFET has a positive threshold voltage with a normally OFF operation. It turns ON when a negative voltage is applied to its gate. These variations cater to different circuit designs and applications.</p></p>
