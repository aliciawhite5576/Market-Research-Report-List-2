<p><strong>The Digital Room Thermostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Millivolt Thermostats</li><li>24 Volt Thermostats</li><li>Line Voltage Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital room thermostat market is categorized into three types: millivolt thermostats, 24-volt thermostats, and line voltage thermostats. Millivolt thermostats are commonly used for gas fireplaces and wall heaters. 24-volt thermostats are compatible with low-voltage systems like central heating and cooling units. Line voltage thermostats are suitable for controlling electric baseboard heaters or other high-voltage systems. Each thermostat type offers different functionalities and is designed to suit specific heating or cooling systems. These options provide consumers with flexibility and a wide range of choices for their digital room thermostat needs.</p></p>
