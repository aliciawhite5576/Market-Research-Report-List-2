<p><p>The Disc Ceramic Capacitors market consists of two main types: Straight Leads Type and Crimped Leads Type. Straight Leads Type capacitors are characterized by leads that are extended in a straight manner from the body of the capacitor. These types are commonly used in applications where soldering is required. On the other hand, Crimped Leads Type capacitors have leads that are crimped or bent at an angle, making them suitable for applications where the capacitor needs to be secured or inserted into a socket. Both types serve different purposes and cater to specific requirements in the market.</p></p>
