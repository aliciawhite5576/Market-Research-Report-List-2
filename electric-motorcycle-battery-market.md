<p><strong>The Electric Motorcycle Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SLI</li><li>AGM</li><li>Lithium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric motorcycle battery market encompasses three main types: SLI (Starting, Lighting, and Ignition) batteries, AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries, and Lithium batteries. SLI batteries are primarily designed for conventional motorcycles and provide a burst of power to start the engine. AGM batteries, on the other hand, offer longer life, faster charging, and higher power output, making them suitable for electric motorcycles. Lithium batteries, known for their lightweight and high energy density, have gained popularity due to their ability to provide extended range, faster charging, and longer lifespan compared to SLI and AGM batteries.</p></p>
