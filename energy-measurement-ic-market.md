<p><p>The Energy Measurement IC market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel. In the single-channel market, energy measurement ICs are designed to measure the energy consumption of a single electrical load or system. This is useful for applications where only one energy source needs to be monitored. On the other hand, the multi-channel market focuses on energy measurement ICs that can measure the energy usage of multiple independent electrical loads or systems simultaneously. This is beneficial for applications requiring the monitoring and analysis of energy consumption in various components or areas.</p></p>
