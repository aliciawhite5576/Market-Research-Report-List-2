Furthermore, technological advancements in enzyme modification techniques and the development of novel enzyme blends are anticipated to drive market growth and foster new product launches.</p><p>In terms of latest market trends, there is a growing application of EMDI in various dairy products such as yogurt, cheese, ice cream, and infant formula.
