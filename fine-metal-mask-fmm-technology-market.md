<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) technology is used in the production of organic light-emitting diode (OLED) displays. Etching is one of the market types where the mask pattern is created by selectively removing material from a metal film to form a stencil. Electroforming, another market type, involves depositing metal onto a substrate to create the desired pattern. Multi Material Composite Method is a market type that combines etching and electroforming, producing a mask with different materials for improved performance. These methods are employed in the manufacturing of FMMs for OLED display production.</p></p>
