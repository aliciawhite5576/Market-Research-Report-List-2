There are two main types of fire extinguisher bomb markets - ignition fire extinguisher bomb and active fire extinguisher bomb. </p><p>The ignition fire extinguisher bomb relies on an ignition source to activate the release of extinguishing agents. This type is commonly used in industrial settings where fire risks are high and can be triggered manually or automatically.</p><p>On the other hand, the active fire extinguisher bomb market offers devices that are equipped with advanced technologies such as heat sensors or smoke detectors. These devices can detect fires and automatically activate the release of extinguishing agents without the need for human intervention.</p><p>Overall, both types of fire extinguisher bomb markets provide effective solutions for fire suppression, with the ignition type requiring manual or automatic activation, while the active type offers automatic detection and activation capabilities.</p></p>
