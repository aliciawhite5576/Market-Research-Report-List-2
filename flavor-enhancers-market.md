Monosodium Glutamate (MSG) is a common flavor enhancer, derived from glutamic acid, that enhances the umami taste. Hydrolyzed Vegetable Protein (HVP) is another type, made from vegetables or soybeans, providing a savory flavor. Yeast Extract, derived from yeast, offers a rich taste and is often used as a natural flavor enhancer. The "Others" category encompasses various flavor enhancers like disodium inosinate and guanylate, providing additional taste enhancement options for food manufacturers.</p></p>
