<p><p>The foot-operated faucet market includes two main types: contact foot pedal and pressed foot pedal. Contact foot pedal faucets require physical contact with the pedal to activate the flow of water. On the other hand, pressed foot pedal faucets activate the water flow when the pedal is pressed down without any physical contact. Both types offer hygienic touchless operation, ideal for settings where hands-free operation is important. These types of foot-operated faucets are gaining popularity due to their convenience and ability to prevent the spread of germs and bacteria.</p></p>
