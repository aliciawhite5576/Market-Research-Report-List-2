<p><p>The Full-Graphic E-Paper ESL (Electronic Shelf Label) market is segmented into various types, including Radio Frequency (RF), Infrared (IR), Near Field Communication (NFC), and Others. RF utilizes radio waves to transmit and receive data, allowing for long-range communication between ESLs and a central system. IR uses infrared light to transmit information, suitable for short-range communication. NFC allows for communication by touching or bringing ESLs close to a reader or smartphone. The "Others" category includes alternative communication technologies specific to certain ESL systems, such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi.</p></p>
