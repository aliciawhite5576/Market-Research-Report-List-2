<p><p>Hazelnut oil is available in different market types, namely roasted hazelnut oil and cold-pressed hazelnut oil. Roasted hazelnut oil is obtained by roasting the hazelnuts before extracting the oil, resulting in a rich and intense flavor. This type of oil is commonly used in cooking and adds a nutty taste to dishes. On the other hand, cold-pressed hazelnut oil is obtained by pressing the hazelnuts without any heat or chemical treatment, preserving its natural aroma and taste. It is often used in cosmetics and food products where a lighter flavor is desired.</p></p>
