<p><ul><li>Straight Moving Type</li><li>Pilot Operated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high pressure solenoid valve market consists of two main types: straight moving type and pilot operated type. The straight moving type operates directly using the electromagnetic force generated by the solenoid coil. It is typically used for applications that require a fast response time. On the other hand, the pilot operated type uses a small sized pilot valve to control the flow of fluid in the main valve. This type is often chosen for applications where high pressure and flow rates are required.</p></p>
