<p><p>The junction box market consists of two main types: non-metallic junction boxes and metallic junction boxes. Non-metallic junction boxes are made of materials such as plastic or fiberglass. They are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and offer good insulation properties. These boxes are commonly used in residential buildings and outdoor installations. On the other hand, metallic junction boxes are typically made of materials like steel or aluminum. They provide more durability, protection against external elements, and are suitable for industrial or commercial applications. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the installation.</p></p>
