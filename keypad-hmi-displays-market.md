<p><p>Keypad HMI displays are a type of human-machine interface used in various industries. The market for these displays can be broadly categorized into two types: resistive and capacitive. Resistive keypad HMI displays use pressure to detect touch and are suitable for environments that may involve gloves or harsh conditions. On the other hand, capacitive keypad HMI displays rely on the conductive properties of the human body and are more responsive to touch. Both types have their advantages and are used in applications such as industrial machinery, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
