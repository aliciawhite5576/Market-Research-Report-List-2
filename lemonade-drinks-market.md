<p><p>Lemonade drinks can be classified into two main types: clear lemonade and cloudy lemonade. Clear lemonade is made using a simple mixture of lemon juice, sugar, and water, resulting in a transparent appearance. On the other hand, cloudy lemonade contains pulp and other particles from the lemon, giving it a more opaque and natural appearance. Both types offer refreshing and tangy flavors, but cloudy lemonade is often perceived as more authentic and homemade, while clear lemonade is often associated with a more commercial and processed product.</p></p>
