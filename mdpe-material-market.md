<p><strong>The MDPE Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene) is a versatile material used in a wide range of applications. The Autoclave Process is a method of manufacturing MDPE that involves high temperature and pressure, resulting in a dense and rigid structure. This process is suitable for producing pipes, fittings, and sheets. On the other hand, the Tubular Process involves extruding the molten MDPE through a circular die, resulting in a tubular shape. This process is more suitable for applications requiring flexible and lightweight MDPE products such as films and packaging materials. Both market types cater to different demands and applications in the MDPE industry.</p></p>
