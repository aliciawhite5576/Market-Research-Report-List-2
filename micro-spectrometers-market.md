<p><p>Micro spectrometers are used for measuring and analyzing the composition of substances. There are two main types of micro spectrometers in the market: chip type and modular type. Chip type micro spectrometers are compact and integrated, with all components included in a single chip. They are cost-effective and suitable for portable devices. On the other hand, modular type micro spectrometers are more flexible, allowing users to customize and upgrade different components to meet specific requirements. They are commonly used in scientific research, industrial laboratories, and quality control applications.</p></p>
