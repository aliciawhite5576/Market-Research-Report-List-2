<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Miniature Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Type</li><li>CCD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for miniature cameras can be categorized into two types based on their image sensor technology: CMOS type and CCD type. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras use a technology that consumes relatively less power, resulting in longer battery life and reduced heat generation. They are widely used in devices like smartphones and webcams due to their lower cost and faster processing capabilities. On the other hand, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras offer higher image quality with improved sensitivity to light, making them suitable for applications like professional photography and surveillance systems that require superior image clarity.</p></p>
