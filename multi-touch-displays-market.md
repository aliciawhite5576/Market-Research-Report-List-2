<p><p>Multi-touch displays are a type of touchscreens that can detect and respond to multiple touch inputs simultaneously. There are three main types of multi-touch displays in the market: </p><p>1. Resistive Multi-touch Type: These displays use multiple layers that are pressed together when touched, allowing the detection of multiple touch points. They are cost-effective but less sensitive to light touch.</p><p>2. Capacitive Multi-touch Type: These displays use the conductive property of the human body to detect touch. They offer enhanced clarity and responsiveness and are commonly used in smartphones and tablets.</p><p>3. Others: This category includes emerging technologies like optical touch, infrared touch, and acoustic touch that are less commonly used but offer unique features in terms of touch detection.</p></p>
