<p><p>Multimode fiber couplers are devices used to split or combine optical signals in optical communication systems. The market types of multimode fiber couplers include 1×2, 1×4, 1×8, and 2×2 couplers. </p><p>A 1×2 fiber coupler enables splitting a single input signal into two output signals. Similarly, the 1×4 coupler splits a single input into four output signals, and 1×8 coupler splits it into eight outputs. On the other hand, a 2×2 coupler combines two input signals into two output signals. These different market types cater to various network configurations and transmission requirements in the optical communication industry.</p></p>
