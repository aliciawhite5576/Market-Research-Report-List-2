<p><p>The natural coconut oil market offers different types of coconut oil, including Virgin Coconut Oil (VCO) and Extra Virgin Coconut Oil (EVCO). VCO is extracted from fresh coconut meat without the use of any chemical solvents or refining processes. It maintains a mild aroma and taste of coconuts. On the other hand, EVCO is also derived from fresh coconut meat but undergoes a cold-pressing process, resulting in a stronger coconut aroma and flavor. Both VCO and EVCO are considered to be of the highest quality and retain the natural benefits of coconut oil.</p></p>
