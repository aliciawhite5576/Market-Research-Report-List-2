<p><p>The NB-IoT Smart Meter market is divided into two types: Single Phase Meter and Three Phase Meter. Single Phase Meters are typically used in residential areas where electricity consumption is relatively low. They are designed to measure the energy consumption of single-phase power supply systems. On the other hand, Three Phase Meters are deployed in commercial or industrial settings where electricity usage is higher. These meters are capable of measuring the energy consumption of three-phase power supply systems, which are commonly found in larger establishments.</p></p>
