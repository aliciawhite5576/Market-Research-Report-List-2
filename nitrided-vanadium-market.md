<p><p>Nitrided vanadium refers to vanadium that has undergone a nitriding process, which enhances its properties for various applications. Two main market types exist for the production of nitrided vanadium: vacuum production process and non-vacuum production process. The vacuum production process involves heating vanadium in a vacuum furnace and introducing nitrogen gas to create nitrided vanadium. In contrast, the non-vacuum production process utilizes a reaction between vanadium and liquid nitrogen to obtain nitrided vanadium. Both processes play a significant role in meeting the demand for high-quality nitrided vanadium in different industries.</p></p>
