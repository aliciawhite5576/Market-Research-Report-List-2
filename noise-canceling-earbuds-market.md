<p><p>The market for noise-canceling earbuds can be divided into two main types: active noise-canceling (ANC) and passive noise-canceling. Active noise-canceling earbuds use technology to actively detect and counter outside noise, creating a quieter listening experience. These earbuds are preferred for environments with high levels of noise. In contrast, passive noise-canceling earbuds rely on physical barriers and insulation to block out external sounds. They are often used in situations where noise levels are relatively low and users desire a more affordable option.</p></p>
