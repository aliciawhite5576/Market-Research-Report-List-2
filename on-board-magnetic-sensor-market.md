<p><strong>The On-Board Magnetic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Sensors</li><li>Magnetoresistive Sensors</li><li>SQUID Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The on-board magnetic sensor market is categorized into various types including Hall effect sensors, magnetoresistive sensors, squid sensors, and others. Hall effect sensors work by detecting changes in the magnetic field through the interaction of electrons. Magnetoresistive sensors measure changes in electrical resistance when exposed to a magnetic field. SQUID sensors use superconducting quantum interference to detect even the smallest magnetic fields. Other types encompass various technologies like fluxgate sensors and magneto-inductive sensors, which function by measuring changes in magnetic flux. Overall, these sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
