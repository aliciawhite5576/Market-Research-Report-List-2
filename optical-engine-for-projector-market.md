<p><p>The optical engine for projector market comprises various types including DLP (digital light processing), LCOS (liquid crystal on silicon), LED (light-emitting diode), and others. DLP projectors use micro mirrors to display images, providing high contrast and brightness. LCOS projectors use liquid crystal technology on a silicon surface, delivering excellent image quality with minimal pixelation. LED projectors employ light-emitting diodes as their light source, resulting in energy efficiency and longer lifespan. Other types include LCD (liquid crystal display) projectors, LBS (laser beam steering) projectors, and laser phosphor projectors, each having its unique features and advantages.</p></p>
