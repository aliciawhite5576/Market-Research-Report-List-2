<p><ul><li>Laminated Particle Board</li><li>Veneered Particle Board</li><li>Cement Bonded Particle Board</li><li>Melamine Particle Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particle board furniture is available in various types in the market. Laminated particle board features a layer of decorative paper on the surface, enhancing its appearance and durability. Veneered particle board is coated with a thin layer of wood veneer, giving it a more natural and high-quality look. Cement bonded particle board is manufactured by combining wood particles with cement, creating a strong and moisture-resistant material. Melamine particle board is coated with melamine resin, offering durability, easy maintenance, and a sleek finish. Overall, these different market types cater to varying consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
