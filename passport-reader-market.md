<p><strong>The Passport Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>Barcode</li><li>OCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passport reader market consists of three main types: RFID, Barcode, and OCR. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) passport readers use radio waves to read and capture information stored in the passport's embedded microchip, offering fast and contactless data retrieval. Barcode passport readers rely on scanning a barcode printed on the passport, allowing for quick and accurate information retrieval. OCR (Optical Character Recognition) passport readers analyze the text and symbols on the passport page using advanced software, providing accurate data extraction even from handwritten or low-quality documents. These types offer various solutions for efficient and secure passport reading.</p></p>
