<p><p>The PE gloves market consists of various types such as Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE), High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), and Ultra-high Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE). LDPE gloves are flexible and offer good chemical resistance, while MDPE gloves have better puncture resistance. HDPE gloves are durable and have a higher melting point. UHMWPE gloves are known for their high strength and abrasion resistance. Each type has unique properties that make them suitable for different applications in industries like healthcare, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
