<p><strong>The Phono Preamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Magnet (MM) Phono Preamps</li><li>Moving Coil (MC) Phono Preamps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phono preamps are devices used to boost and equalize the low-level analog signal from turntables. There are two main types: Moving Magnet (MM) and Moving Coil (MC). MM phono preamps are more common and affordable, as they work with most cartridges. MC phono preamps, on the other hand, are designed for high-end turntables with specialized cartridges. They provide superior sound quality, but tend to be more expensive. Both types serve to amplify the turntable's signal and ensure accurate playback of vinyl records.</p></p>
