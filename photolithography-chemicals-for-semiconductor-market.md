<p><p>Photolithography chemicals for the semiconductor market include various types such as silicon wafer, photoresist, HMDS (hexamethyldisilazane), photoresist ancillaries, and others. Silicon wafers are the base material on which semiconductor devices are fabricated. Photoresist is a light-sensitive material used to transfer the desired patterns onto the silicon wafers. HMDS is a surface treatment chemical that enables better adhesion of photoresist. Photoresist ancillaries include chemicals used in the preparation and development of photoresist. Other chemicals in this market serve various purposes, such as cleaning, etching, or protecting the wafers during the fabrication process.</p></p>
