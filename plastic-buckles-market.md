<p><p>The plastic buckles market can be divided into three types. Firstly, side release buckles are designed to release by squeezing both sides of the buckle. They are commonly used in applications such as backpacks, belts, and outdoor gear. Secondly, center push and cam buckles are operated by pushing a central button or lever, allowing for easy and quick release. They are often used in webbing straps and luggage straps. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of plastic buckles that do not fit into the previous two categories, such as ladder buckles or loop buckles.</p></p>
