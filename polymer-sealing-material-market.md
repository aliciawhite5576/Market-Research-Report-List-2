<p><p>Polymer sealing materials are used in various markets, including hydraulic and pneumatic seals, rotary seals, and spring-powered seals. Hydraulic and pneumatic seals are employed in systems that use fluid power to transmit force, ensuring leakage prevention. Rotary seals provide a barrier against the ingress of dust and fluids in rotating equipment. Spring-powered seals use the energy from a spring to create a tight seal, commonly found in valves and connectors. These different types of polymer sealing materials cater to specific applications, ensuring efficient and reliable performance in various industries.</p></p>
