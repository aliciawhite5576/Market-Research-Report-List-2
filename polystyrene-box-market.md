<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Polystyrene boxes are a popular choice for packaging and insulation purposes due to their thermal properties. Open cell foam polystyrene boxes have interconnected air-filled pockets, providing better cushioning and flexibility for fragile items. They are commonly used for delicate electronics or glassware. On the other hand, closed cell foam polystyrene boxes have sealed cells, making them denser and more rigid. They offer higher insulation and structural strength, making them suitable for perishable goods transportation or construction use where durability is crucial. The choice between the two depends on specific requirements and intended application.</p></p>
