<p><p>Prepaid gift cards are categorized into two main market types: open loop and closed loop. Open loop gift cards are issued by financial institutions and can be used anywhere that accepts credit cards. They offer flexibility and can be loaded with any desired amount. In contrast, closed loop gift cards are issued by specific retailers or businesses and can only be used at their establishments. These cards often have fixed denominations and are intended for specific purchases. Both types serve as convenient and popular gifting options.</p></p>
