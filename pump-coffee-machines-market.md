<p><p>Pump coffee machines are categorized into two market types: manual coffee machines and automatic coffee machines. Manual coffee machines require the user to manually operate the pump to create the pressure needed for brewing coffee. These machines offer more control and customization options but require more skill and effort. On the other hand, automatic coffee machines have built-in pumps that automatically create the required pressure for brewing. These machines are more convenient and user-friendly, as they require less manual intervention. Both types of machines cater to different consumer preferences and brewing styles.</p></p>
