<p><p>The semiconductor test probe cards market includes four main types: cantilever probe cards, vertical probe cards, MEMS probe cards, and others. Cantilever probe cards use flexible beams to make contact with the device under test. Vertical probe cards have vertically positioned probes for testing. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) probe cards utilize micro-sized probes for increased testing accuracy. The "others" category encompasses specialized probe cards such as RF (radio frequency) probe cards or high-density probe cards. These types of probe cards are crucial in testing the functionality and performance of semiconductor devices during the manufacturing process.</p></p>
