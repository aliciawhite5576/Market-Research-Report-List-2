<p><p>Signal boosters, also known as cell phone signal repeaters or amplifiers, are devices that improve cellular signal strength in areas with weak or poor network coverage. There are two main types of signal boosters: analog and digital. Analog boosters amplify the existing analog signals, which were widely used in older cellular networks. On the other hand, digital signal boosters enhance the newer digital signals that are used in modern cell networks. Both types serve the purpose of improving signal strength, but analog boosters are suitable for older networks while digital boosters are designed for modern networks.</p></p>
