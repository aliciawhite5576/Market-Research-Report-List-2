<p><p>Silicon Steel Rolls market consists of two main types: Oriented Electrical Steel and Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel. Oriented Electrical Steel is a material specifically designed for use in transformers and electrical machines, as it exhibits high magnetic properties. It has a highly developed grain structure that allows for effective magnetic alignment. On the other hand, Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel is used in the production of motors, generators, and other electrical equipment. It lacks a specific grain orientation, making it suitable for applications requiring a diverse magnetic performance.</p></p>
