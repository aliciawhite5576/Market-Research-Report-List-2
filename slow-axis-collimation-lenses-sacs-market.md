<p><p>Slow Axis Collimation (SAC) lenses are designed to collimate the slow axis of diode laser beams. The market for SAC lenses can be categorized based on the Numerical Aperture (NA). Lenses with NA=0.09 have a larger acceptance angle, allowing them to collimate light from diode lasers with larger beam divergences. NA=0.065 lenses have a medium acceptance angle. NA=0.05 lenses have a smaller acceptance angle, suitable for diode lasers with smaller beam divergences. The "Others" market category includes SAC lenses with different NA values or unique specifications that cater to specific customer requirements.</p></p>
