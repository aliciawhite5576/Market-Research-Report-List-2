<p><p>Small pitch LED display market types refer to different sizes and resolutions of LED screens used for displaying content with high precision and clarity. The types include P2.1-P2.5 mm, P1.7-P2.0 mm, P1.3-P1.69 mm, P1.0-P1.29 mm, and below P1mm. These specifications indicate the distance between each pixel on the display, with smaller numbers representing higher pixel density and better image quality. Therefore, below P1mm displays offer the highest resolution and image sharpness, making them suitable for applications demanding exceptional visual performance.</p></p>
