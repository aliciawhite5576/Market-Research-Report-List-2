<p><p>Smart cash registers are modernized versions of traditional cash registers that offer advanced functionalities. There are two market types based on the number of screens: single screen and double screen. In a single screen market, the cash register has one integrated display where the cashier can input transactions and view the total amount. In a double screen market, the cash register is equipped with two screens, allowing the cashier to face one display towards themselves for inputting data, while the other screen faces the customer, displaying the transaction details for review.</p></p>
