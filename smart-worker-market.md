<p><p>The Smart Worker market can be categorized into three types: BLE/Bluetooth, LPWAN, and WFAN. BLE/Bluetooth technology enables short-range wireless communication between devices, allowing workers to connect and interact with various smart devices in their vicinity. LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network) enables long-range connectivity with low power consumption, facilitating communication between remote workers and central systems. WFAN (Wireless Fidelity Ad-Hoc Network) provides a wireless network for real-time communication among workers, enabling collaboration and coordination in dynamic work environments. All these technologies aim to enhance worker productivity and connectivity in different contexts.</p></p>
