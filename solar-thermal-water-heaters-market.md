<p><p>Solar thermal water heaters are devices that use sunlight to heat water for domestic or commercial use. Two types of solar water heaters available in the market are pressure-bearing and non-pressure solar water heaters. Pressure-bearing solar water heaters are designed to withstand high pressures and are suitable for areas with fluctuating water pressure. On the other hand, non-pressure solar water heaters are more affordable and less complex, making them suitable for areas with stable water pressure. Both types offer cost savings and environmental benefits compared to traditional water heating methods.</p></p>
