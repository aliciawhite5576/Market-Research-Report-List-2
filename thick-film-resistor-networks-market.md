<p><ul><li>Single-In-Line</li><li>Dual-In-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thick Film Resistor Networks market includes various types such as Single-In-Line (SIL) and Dual-In-Line (DIL). SIL resistor networks are composed of a single line of resistors, making them compact and suitable for space-constrained applications. They are typically used in low-density circuits. On the other hand, DIL resistor networks consist of two parallel rows of resistors, allowing for higher resistance values and increased circuit density. DIL networks are commonly used in more complex electronic systems where more resistors are needed in a compact form.</p></p>
