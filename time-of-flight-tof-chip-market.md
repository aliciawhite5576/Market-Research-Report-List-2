<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) chip market can be categorized into indirect and direct types. Indirect ToF chips utilize additional components like light emitters and detectors to measure the time it takes for light to bounce back from objects. These chips are commonly used in applications such as range finding and gesture recognition. On the other hand, direct ToF chips integrate the light emitter and detector in a single chip, enabling more compact design and simplified manufacturing. Direct ToF chips are typically used in applications like 3D imaging, augmented reality, and autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
