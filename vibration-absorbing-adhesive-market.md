<p><strong>The Vibration-absorbing Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Type</li><li>Shear Type</li><li>Compound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration-absorbing adhesive market comprises three main types: compression type, shear type, and compound type. Compression type adhesives help absorb vibrations by compressing elastomeric materials between two surfaces, minimizing the impact. Shear type adhesives work by spreading vibrations across a larger contact area, reducing their intensity. Compound type adhesives combine compression and shear mechanisms to achieve effective vibration absorption. These types cater to various applications requiring vibration reduction, providing engineering solutions in sectors such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
