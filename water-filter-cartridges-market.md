<p><p>The water filter cartridges market consists of various types of cartridges designed to remove impurities from water. Ceramic filter cartridges are made of porous materials that trap contaminants and bacteria in the water. Reverse osmosis membrane cartridges use a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved solids and particles. Activated carbon filter cartridges contain carbon granules that adsorb chemicals and organic compounds. Other types include UV filters, alkaline filters, and sediment filters. These cartridges are crucial for providing clean and safe drinking water in households, industries, and commercial settings.</p></p>
