<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water massage tables market can be categorized into two types: open type and closed type. Open type water massage tables have a design where the user lies on top of the table with a water-filled mattress underneath. The user comes into direct contact with water during the massage session. In contrast, closed type water massage tables have a sealed compartment filled with warm water, where the user enters like a bathtub. The closed type ensures complete privacy and a more immersive experience for the user.</p></p>
